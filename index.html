About the Project

The EmpowerHer project is designed to address the issue of low participation rates in physical activities among females. By creating a safe, inclusive, and engaging environment, we aim to boost confidence and enjoyment in multi-activity sports. This initiative focuses on understanding barriers, implementing supportive policies, and evaluating outcomes to ensure long-term impact.
